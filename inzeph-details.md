## ENTERPRISE & AI PORTFOLIO
### Proven Enterprise & AI Portfolio

**Eyebrow Label:** Track Record

---

### Project 1: AppsInfy — Enterprise Work Intelligence Platform
- **Category:** Enterprise Operations Integration
- **Problem:** Heavy-industry enterprises (Oil & Gas/Mining) struggling with disconnected legacy systems, manual workflows, and severe data silos causing compliance risks.
- **Solution:** Built a fully integrated digital operations platform (Go backend + Flutter hybrid app), unifying fragmented workflows into one interface tied securely to Windows Active Directory.
- **Results:**
  - Audit Prep Speed: Slashed from 3 weeks to instant automated generation.
  - API Latency: Dropped by 65% via Go concurrency models.
  - Data Accuracy: Minimized manual-entry anomalies by 98%.

---

### Project 2: Owlo — The 24/7 AI Tutor
- **Category:** AI Learning Ecosystem
- **Problem:** The Availability Gap: Providing every student with a dedicated tutor at any hour. Human tutoring is too expensive to scale, and static tools lack the nuance of voice-led education.
- **Solution:** A Multi-Modal Ecosystem featuring Nemo — a two-way voice tutor powered by Whisper and GPT-4o. Includes 4 distinct AI flashcard types, 6 note-taking formats, and a gamified achievements system.
- **Results:**
  - Database Overhead: 100% elimination of separate Vector DB costs by unifying embeddings in pgvector.
  - Query Speed: Real-time similarity search processing under 120ms.
  - Student Engagement: 3x increase in session duration via two-way voice.

---

### Project 3: BeyondAsk — The Digital Twin
- **Category:** Enterprise RAG Infrastructure
- **Problem:** The writing sounds robotic. The goal was to build an AI that writes exactly like a specific person — capturing their unique tone, phrases, and 'linguistic DNA'.
- **Solution:** Developed the core Python intelligence engine — 'Digital Brain' using LangChain RAG, while providing technical leadership to their internal team for application development. Designed the central gateway with Smart Routing to optimize AI model selection.
- **Results:**
  - System Uptime: 99.99% reliability through multi-provider failover routing.
  - Operating Costs: Reduced token waste by 40% using precision Smart Routing.
  - Scalability: Handles 10k+ concurrent enterprise requests without degradation.

## SLIDE 6 — AI QUERY ENGINE
### Engineering the Vision: The Intelligent Query Layer

**Eyebrow Label:** The AI Engine

**Intro Statement:**
Traditional databases look for exact text matches. We don't use traditional searches.

---

### How It Works — 3 Steps

#### Step 1: Vector Embeddings
Historical design assets and floor plans are converted into mathematical coordinates (vectors) — enabling geometric comparison at machine speed.

#### Step 2: Semantic Understanding
Our Smart Routing engine (leveraging models like GPT-4o) understands the intent and geometry behind the words — not just the spelling.

#### Step 3: High-Precision Matching
Using pgvector, the system retrieves the exact 3D asset matching the 2D requirement instantly, eliminating hours of manual digging.
